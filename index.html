Frequently Asked Questions FAQs

What is QWQER merchant delivery service?
+

QWQER merchant delivery service helps businesses manage customer orders, parcels, stock movement and recurring deliveries through instant, same-day, next-day and bulk delivery options in serviceable areas.

Which businesses can partner with QWQER Pakistan?
+

Ecommerce sellers, retailers, fashion brands, food businesses, pharmacies, grocery sellers, gift shops, document delivery users and local businesses can use QWQER for regular delivery coordination.

Does QWQER support same-day delivery in Lahore?
+

Yes. QWQER supports same-day delivery in Lahore depending on pickup location, drop-off location, rider availability and serviceable route conditions.

Can merchants send bulk deliveries through QWQER?
+

Yes. QWQER supports bulk delivery and multiple-order movement for merchants that need planned dispatch coordination.

Does QWQER provide live tracking?
+

Yes. QWQER provides tracking support so merchants and customers can follow delivery progress from pickup to drop-off.

How can a business start with QWQER?
+

A business can submit the merchant inquiry form with contact details, pickup area, monthly order volume and delivery needs. The QWQER team can then guide onboarding and next steps.
